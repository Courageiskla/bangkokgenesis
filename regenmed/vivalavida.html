Recharge Your Life, Intravenously

Viva La Vida is a holistic regenerative program featuring customized intravenous (IV) vitamin drips. This method delivers essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants directly into your bloodstream, ensuring 99% absorption for immediate and powerful effects. Unlike oral supplements, IV therapy bypasses the digestive system, providing your body with the direct nourishment it needs to restore health, boost energy, and combat the effects of a demanding lifestyle. This treatment can be administered every 1-2 weeks, or as recommended by our physicians, for optimal well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

IV therapy delivers nutrients directly into the bloodstream, guaranteeing up to 99% absorption. Oral supplements must pass through the digestive system, where a significant portion of their potency is lost. This direct delivery means faster, more effective, and more noticeable results.

For optimal and sustained results, we generally recommend a session every 1-2 weeks. However, our doctor will consult with you to create a personalized treatment plan based on your individual health assessment and wellness goals.

The procedure is very comfortable. You may feel a slight pinch when the small needle is inserted, similar to a standard blood draw, but the infusion itself is painless. IV vitamin therapy is very safe when administered by medical professionals. Side effects are rare but may include minor bruising at the injection site. Our team will review your medical history to ensure your complete safety.
