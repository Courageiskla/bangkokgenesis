A Legacy of Excellence & Global Mastery

Dr. Pume's journey is one of continuous and distinguished scholarship. His foundational expertise was forged at the prestigious Faculty of Medicine, Ramathibodi Hospital, and further honed through elite training programs in cosmetic dermatology and laser therapy. He is a master of the most advanced aesthetic techniques, from the intricate art of 6D-Rhinoplasty and advanced midface dermal filler injections to achieving full-face harmony with botulinum toxin and bio-stimulators.

Beyond aesthetic refinement, Dr. Pume is a board-certified luminary in the science of longevity. With a coveted certification from the American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine (A4M), he pioneers regenerative strategies and advanced therapies that restore vitality from the cellular level. His pursuit of knowledge is global; Dr. Pume is a distinguished presence at the world's most influential aesthetic congresses, from the International Congress of Aesthetic Dermatology (ICAD) to the World Congress of Aesthetic Surgery & Medicine (WCAM), ensuring he brings the future of aesthetic and regenerative medicine to his patients today.
