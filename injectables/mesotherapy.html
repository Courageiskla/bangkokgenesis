Tailored Solutions for Radiant Skin

Mesotherapy is a versatile program tailored to your specific skin needs. It's broadly divided into two main groups: Mesoskin, which focuses on improving skin complexion and texture, and Mesofat, designed for reducing localized fat deposits. By delivering potent ingredients directly into the skin's middle layer, Mesotherapy provides targeted results for a wide range of concerns. Treatments can be administered every 1-2 weeks, or as recommended by our physicians, for optimal outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Mesotherapy focuses on skin rejuvenation by delivering a cocktail of vitamins, minerals, and other active ingredients into the superficial layers of the skin to improve texture, tone, and radiance. Fillers, on the other hand, are designed to add volume, contour, and fill in deep wrinkles by injecting a gel-like substance (usually hyaluronic acid) deeper into the skin.

For optimal and sustained results, we generally recommend an initial series of treatments every 1-2 weeks. After the initial series, maintenance sessions may be scheduled further apart. Our doctor will consult with you to create a personalized treatment plan based on your skin condition and wellness goals.

The procedure is very comfortable. We apply a topical anesthetic cream before the treatment to minimize discomfort. You might feel a series of tiny pricks, but it is generally well-tolerated. There is minimal to no downtime; you may experience some mild redness or tiny bumps at the injection sites, which typically subside within 24 hours.
