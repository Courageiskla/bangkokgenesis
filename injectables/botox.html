Botox

A safe and effective solution to smooth away wrinkles, refine facial contours, and achieve a naturally youthful appearance.

What is Botox?

Botox is a purified protein used to temporarily relax facial muscles. At Bangkok Genesis Clinic, we view it as a medical art form. By precisely targeting muscles, we smooth dynamic wrinkles—lines from expressions like smiling or frowning—and sculpt features like the jawline for a natural, refreshed look.

The Procedure

Our process is designed for your comfort, safety, and optimal results.

1. Consultation

We discuss your goals and create a personalized plan.

2. Preparation

We cleanse the area and mark precise injection points.

3. Injection

A quick 10-15 minute process with an ultra-fine needle.

4. Completion

Receive aftercare instructions and return to your day.

Preparation

  • ✓ Avoid blood thinners (Aspirin, Vitamin E) for one week.
  • ✓ Refrain from alcohol 24 hours prior to treatment.
  • ✓ Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face.

Aftercare

  • ✓ Remain upright for at least 4 hours post-treatment.
  • ✓ Avoid strenuous exercise and heat for 24 hours.
  • ✓ Do not rub or massage the treated areas.
