Oligio Lifting

Experience the next generation of RF skin tightening. Oligio offers a comfortable, non-invasive solution to redefine your contours, smooth wrinkles, and restore youthful vitality with advanced monopolar radiofrequency technology.

What is Oligio? The Power of Monopolar RF

Oligio is an innovative, non-invasive skin tightening and lifting device utilizing **Monopolar Radiofrequency (RF)** technology. Unlike conventional RF, Oligio delivers a uniform, controlled heat deep into the dermal layers and subcutaneous fat. This thermal energy targets collagen fibers, causing them to contract for an immediate tightening effect, while simultaneously stimulating fibroblasts to produce new, healthy collagen and elastin over time. This dual action leads to a natural, long-lasting rejuvenation, improving skin elasticity, firmness, and overall texture.

Equipped with intelligent sensors and a built-in cooling system (cooling shot and vibration), Oligio ensures a precise and comfortable treatment experience. It effectively addresses concerns like sagging skin, fine lines, wrinkles, and lack of facial definition on the face, neck, and even around the eyes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Oligio is designed for comfort. Most patients report a warm, tolerable sensation. The device's integrated cooling and vibration system minimizes discomfort throughout the procedure, making it a very manageable experience. Some areas might feel warmer than others, but it should not be painful.

Oligio vs. Ulthera: Oligio uses Monopolar RF to heat the dermis for overall skin tightening and texture improvement, focusing on collagen stimulation. Ulthera uses Microfocused Ultrasound (MFU-V) to target the deeper SMAS layer for a more pronounced lift, specifically addressing structural laxity.

Oligio vs. Thermage: Both use Monopolar RF technology. Oligio is a newer generation device, offering enhanced patient comfort due to its advanced cooling and vibration features, and often allows for faster treatment times with effective results.

You may notice an immediate subtle tightening due to collagen contraction. However, the true results develop gradually over 2-3 months as your body naturally produces new collagen. Results typically last for 6-12 months, and some patients find annual maintenance treatments beneficial to prolong the effects.

Oligio is ideal for individuals experiencing mild to moderate skin laxity, fine lines, and wrinkles. It is suitable for those looking for a non-surgical solution to improve skin firmness and overall texture on the face, neck, and around the eyes. A consultation with our doctor will determine if Oligio is the right treatment for you.
