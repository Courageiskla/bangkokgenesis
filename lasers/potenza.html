Lasers & EBDs

Potenza™

The world's first 4-mode RF Microneedling device. Unlock your skin's potential for a firmer, brighter, and radically renewed complexion.

A New Era of Skin Revitalization

Potenza is a revolutionary RF (Radiofrequency) Microneedling treatment that takes skin rejuvenation to the next level. By delivering focused RF energy through ultrafine needles, it triggers the body's natural healing process, powerfully stimulating new collagen and elastin production. This process addresses signs of aging from the inside out, rebuilding the skin's core structure for results that are both dramatic and remarkably natural. It's a customizable treatment for all skin types, on any part of the body, any time of year.

The Power of 4-Mode Technology

Potenza is the world's first and only RF microneedling device to combine Monopolar and Bipolar RF in one system, offering unparalleled treatment customization.

Monopolar RF

Delivers energy uniformly across a large, deep area of tissue for significant skin tightening and body contouring.

Bipolar RF

Concentrates energy in a more superficial zone between the needle electrodes, perfect for revitalizing the skin and treating textural issues.

Fusion & Tiger Tip™

Specialized tips like the Fusion Tip enhance topical penetration, while Tiger Tip™ technology allows for treating more tissue at multiple depths simultaneously.

Your Journey to Radiant Skin

Our process is designed for comfort, safety, and superior results.

1. Consultation 🧑‍⚕️

We discuss your goals and analyze your skin to create a personalized treatment plan.

2. Preparation 🧼

Your skin is cleansed, and a topical numbing cream is applied for maximum comfort.

3. RF Microneedling ✨

The Potenza device delivers precise RF energy, creating micro-channels and stimulating collagen.

4. Renewal 💖

Results emerge over weeks as collagen rebuilds, revealing smoother, firmer skin.

Preparation 📋

  • ✓ Avoid exfoliants (like retinol, AHA/BHA) for 5-7 days prior.
  • ✓ Stay hydrated and avoid excessive sun exposure.
  • ✓ Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face on treatment day.

Aftercare ☀️

  • ✓ Expect mild redness and swelling for 24-48 hours, like a mild sunburn.
  • ✓ Avoid strenuous exercise, saunas, and swimming for 48 hours.
  • ✓ Use a gentle cleanser and moisturizer. Avoid active ingredients for a week.
  • ✓ Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ sunscreen daily is essential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Results are progressive. You may notice some initial tightening, but optimal results typically appear around 4-6 weeks after your final treatment session as your body rebuilds its collagen supply. Results can continue to improve for up to 12 months.

While both treatments use needles to stimulate collagen, Potenza adds Radiofrequency (RF) energy. This heat supercharges the collagen and elastin production, leading to significantly more powerful skin tightening and contouring results compared to the more superficial improvements from traditional microneedling.

We apply a strong topical numbing cream before the procedure to ensure your comfort. Most patients report feeling only a light pressure and a mild heating sensation during the treatment. Your comfort is our priority.

For best results, we typically recommend a series of 3-4 treatments, spaced about 4-6 weeks apart. However, the exact number will be tailored to your specific skin concerns and desired outcome during your personal consultation.
